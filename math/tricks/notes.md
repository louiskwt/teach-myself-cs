Here is an interesting trick:

- Think of a number
- Double your number
- Add 4
- Divide by 2
- Subtract the original number

No matter what your original number is, you get 2 at the end! 

Show that this is the case.

2x+4/2 - x = 2

2x+4/2 = x + 2

2x + 4 = 2x + 4

x = x
